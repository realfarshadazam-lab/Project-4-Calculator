Lakers Flexbox Fan Page

This is a responsive Flexbox layout using a Lakers theme. I really tried to make it aesthetic while making it work and mesh well. It shows different row designs for desktop, tablet, and mobile, and it also uses the CSS order property to change item position on smaller screens.

Team Identity

The Lakers are one of the most famous teams in the NBA. Also my favorite team of all time. They are known for their history, championships, and purple and gold colors.

Third Row

Season Outlook

This section is wider on desktop, so it takes up more space than the card on the left. I used this row to show a 1 to 3 Flexbox layout.

On tablet and mobile, the two sections will stack on top of each other so the content is easier to read on smaller screens.

Fourth Row

Full Width Spotlight

This row is one full-width item. It stretches across the whole container and shows how a single Flexbox item can take up the entire row.

Defense

This is one of the smaller side cards in the row. It helps show the 1 : 2 : 1 layout on desktop.

Fifth Row

Main Story

This center card is larger than the two side cards, so on desktop the row becomes 1 : 2 : 1.

When the screen gets smaller, I used the order property so this main card can appear first.

Offense

This is the other smaller side card. Together with the middle card, it creates a different Flexbox row from the ones above.
